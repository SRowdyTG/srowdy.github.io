Greenster

Game created on Construct 2 for my Multimedia Art course.

The player takes on the role of an explorer who has entered the forest of the Greenster, a monster that will persue him until he escapes or is captured by it.

The explorer must collect a number of rocks that have the power to weaken the Greenster, jumping around and using abilities to evade it. Currently two levels available.

Design by @RodrigosCreed

Gameshow

Text adventure created on C++ for my General Computing course.

Gameshow is a simple text adventure where the player is thrown on a live gameshow, and must take different decisions that will decide their prize upon completion.

There's the posibility to get the maximum prize, different amounts of cash in between, or total failure, depending on the player's decisions. Some cool ASCII art included.
