Occult

Competitive map created for the "Beneath the Dust" 2015 mapping constest.

Almost completely underground, Occult consists of 3 different areas connected with each other through tunnels. A clandestine weapons market, a secret monastery and a sewer system.

Occult had the objective of being a competitively viable map. I was tested with multiple different players in competitive play to correct all bugs and polish all game mechanics possible. It's one of my biggest and proudest projects so far.

Design by @RodrigosCreed

Station

Compact map for training and team deathmatch matches. Set on a space station somewhere in the universe...

Station Station is a small map that can be played to train with bots or for multiplayer team deathmatch. Almost all textures are custom made.

It served as an experiment to test the popularity of 'aim' maps vs. regular maps.
